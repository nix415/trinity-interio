What We Offer

Our Services

Approach

Tailored to Your Vision

Every client and every space is different. Our service offerings are structured to meet you where you are — whether you need a partner from the very beginning or expert guidance at a specific stage of your project.

All services are available in Los Angeles and throughout California. International projects considered by inquiry.

Our Offerings

01

Full Design

Our most comprehensive service — end-to-end design partnership from the first concept through final installation. We manage every detail so you don't have to.

  • Discovery & brief development
  • Concept board & spatial planning
  • Furniture selection & custom sourcing
  • Material & finish specification
  • Contractor coordination
  • Full installation & styled reveal
Begin a Project
02

Bespoke Consultation

A focused design session — one to three hours — for targeted guidance on materials, furniture, color strategy, spatial flow, or a specific room. Perfect for clients with a strong vision who need expert direction to refine it.

  • Pre-session questionnaire
  • On-site or virtual meeting
  • Material & furniture recommendations
  • Written summary & mood board
Book a Session
03

Renovation Direction

We partner with your architect and contractor team to ensure the design vision is honored at every stage of construction — from finish selection through to the final walkthrough.

  • Design intent documentation
  • Finish & material schedules
  • Weekly site walkthroughs
  • Contractor briefing & liaison
  • Punch list & final sign-off
Enquire
04

Home Staging

We transform vacant and occupied properties into aspirational spaces that photograph beautifully and command higher offers. Our staging draws on the same design vocabulary as our full residential projects — curated furniture, intentional styling, editorial finishing — so every listing tells a compelling story from the first image.

  • Vacant & occupied staging
  • Listing photography prep & art direction
  • Furniture & accessory sourcing
  • Pre-market refresh consultation
Inquire About Staging
05

Airbnb & STR Design

Short-term rental guests expect a hotel experience in a private home. We design Airbnb and vacation rental properties to maximize bookings, command premium nightly rates, and generate five-star reviews — balancing durability and easy maintenance with a look that stands out in any listing market.

  • Full STR & Airbnb interior design
  • Furniture & decor procurement
  • Listing photography direction
  • Refresh & rebrand of existing rentals
