The People Behind the Work

About Us

Founder

Designed with
Intention

Our studio is led by a team of designers who believe that luxury is not defined by price, but by the quality of thought behind every decision. We are obsessive about proportion, light, and the life lived inside a room.

Trinity was built on the conviction that the best interiors feel less designed and more discovered — as if the space had always been this way, and only needed the right hands to reveal it.

"We don't decorate rooms. We compose lives."

— Founder, Trinity Interior Design
