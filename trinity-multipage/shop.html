About the Edit

Objects Worth Living With

The Trinity Edit is not a collection of things. It is a collection of decisions — pieces that have earned a place in the room through beauty, craft, or both.

Every object we carry comes from makers we trust: small workshops, independent artisans, and heritage producers who still make things by hand. We take no shortcuts with quality, and we take none here.
